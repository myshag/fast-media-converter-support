About

Fast Media Converter converts images, PDFs, videos, and audio on your device — JPG/PNG/HEIC, PDF ↔ images, MP4/MOV compression, and audio extraction to M4A/WAV. Everything runs locally; your files never leave your phone.

FAQ

How many conversions are free?
The free plan includes 10 conversions per day, all formats, no watermark — free forever. Pro unlocks unlimited conversions with a single one-time purchase (no subscription).

Where do my converted files go?
You can save results to Photos or Files, or share them. Use “Save all” to export a batch at once.

Why does a PDF convert only the first 5 pages?
That’s the free limit per PDF. Pro converts all pages, and you can pick exactly which pages to convert.

I bought Pro on another device.
Tap “Restore Purchases” on the Pro screen — it’s tied to your Apple ID.
